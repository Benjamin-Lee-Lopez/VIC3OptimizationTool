Victoria 3 Optimization Tool is meant to be a personal long term project to assist with the heavy calculations involved with each playthrough.

The tool will also serve as a learning sandbox, as the economy is never so simple, but Victoria 3 allows for intersectionality of many variables within a sandbox enviornment.

The main goal is to help try and see how much of the purely mathematical questions can be offloaded and automated. Balancing budgets, managing deficit spending, and predicting the effect of new production methods, are just a few examples of questions I plan to expedite the answer to.

At the moment, the plan is to allow for manual input of certain metrics, and then outputting how both the immediate and reverberating effects will impact the nation in question. However, on a larger scale, I would hope to take current save game information in order to answer those questions with more accuracy, and to take in further inputs, such as market value of goods, wages, obsessions & taboos, and any more niche variables.

As it stands, the tool is able to calculate the weekly budget, current GDP, and the % of expected GDP increse, and tell you if your debt will outpace your debt ceiling and could cause a default, if we are playing it safe and underspending, or if we are in the sweet spot and matching our debt ceiling pace.

This metric would be important as spending typically means you are building more, and efficiently boosting your nation's economy faster than your neighbors. On top of this, debt is actually distributed back to certain members of the population, which can find its way back into the country funds through further investment. The tool can't tell you if this is how you should play it, but it will inform you where you are trending.
